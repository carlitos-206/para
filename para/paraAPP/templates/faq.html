Para is a mobile application that enables you to see more data about your orders so that you can pick the best orders for you.

With Tip Transparency, we show you the tip amount that delivery platforms are hiding.

Para is completely free. We do not keep or sell any of your data.

We’re a venture-backed startup and are finalizing our business model so that we don’t need to charge drivers nor sell any data.

If you like it, please leave us an app review or tell your friends about it!
